My Story

The Journey That Shaped Who I Am Today

Hello, I’m Faisal Al-Ali, a 22-year-old Computer Science graduate based in Jordan. From a young age, I’ve been captivated by the endless possibilities that technology offers. My journey into this field wasn’t straightforward—it was shaped by unexpected inspirations and a passion for creativity.

Growing up, I was an avid gamer. Video games weren’t just entertainment for me; they were worlds to explore, challenges to overcome, and puzzles to solve. I was fascinated by how games worked behind the scenes—how complex systems could create immersive experiences that brought people together.

Despite this deep connection with technology through gaming, Computer Science wasn’t my first pick. It wasn’t until I began experimenting with coding and building simple projects during my teenage years that I realized the parallels between gaming and programming. Both involve creativity, logic, and a constant drive to improve. That realization set me on the path to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Throughout my academic journey, I’ve worked on developing user-friendly websites, designing algorithms, and collaborating on team projects. These experiences helped me see the bigger picture: technology is not just about building; it’s about solving real-world problems and making a positive impact.

Today, I’m particularly interested in web development, software engineering, and exploring emerging technologies like artificial intelligence and machine learning. My ultimate goal is to merge my love for creativity and logic to contribute to projects that make a difference in people’s lives.

When I’m not coding, you’ll still find me gaming, exploring new tools, or brainstorming my next big idea. Gaming remains a part of my story—it reminds me of where I started and continues to fuel my passion for creating and learning.

This is just the beginning of my story, and I’m excited to see where this journey will take me. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me.
